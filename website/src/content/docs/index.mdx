## What are Behave Graphs?

Behave Graphs provide a powerful and intuitive way to define, visualize, and execute complex behaviors through a modular, graph-based approach. Whether you're building AI agents, game logic, or intricate workflows, Behave Graphs help you break down complexity into manageable, reusable nodes.
